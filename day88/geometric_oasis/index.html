Circle

A circle is a plane figure bounded by one curved line, and such that all straight lines drawn from a certain point within it to the bounding line, are equal.

Square

A square is a polygon whose edges are great circle arcs of equal distance, which meet at equal angles.

Triangle

A triangle is a polygon with three edges and three vertices. It is one of the basic shapes in geometry

Rectangle

A rectangle is a special case of a parallelogram in which each pair of adjacent sides is perpendicular.

Parallelogram

A parallelogram is a simple (non-self-intersecting) quadrilateral with two pairs of parallel sides.

Trapezoid

A Trapezoid is a convex quadrilateral with at least one pair of parallel sides.
